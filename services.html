Extension Methods

Tape Applications

Tape-In Extensions

Lightweight and natural-looking tape wefts that blend seamlessly with your natural hair. The most popular choice for adding volume and length quickly. Medical-grade adhesive ensures secure, comfortable wear.

30–60 min install · 6–8 wk maintenance
Sewn-In Wefts

Genius Weft

The premium weft option — a hybrid of hand-tied and machine-made construction. Ultra-thin, beard-free edges that lay completely flat. Ideal for fine to medium hair seeking maximum natural movement.

1.5–2.5 hr install · 6–8 wk maintenance
Keratin Bonds

K-Tip Extensions

Individual strand extensions bonded with Italian keratin for a completely natural look. Keratin bonds provide exceptional hold while remaining invisible. Perfect for adding density and seamless length.

3–6 hr install · 6–8 wk maintenance
Instant Extensions

Clip-In Extensions

Premium clip-in extensions for special occasions or everyday wear. 100% virgin human hair clips that attach in minutes, give massive volume, and remove just as easily. Zero commitment, maximum impact.

Under 30 min · No maintenance
Cold Fusion

I-Tip (Micro Bead)

Heat-free application using tiny silicone-lined micro beads. No chemicals, no adhesive — just pure hair clamped strand by strand for the most natural finish. Excellent for fine or sensitive hair.

1.5–3 hr install · 6–8 wk maintenance
Halo & Ponytail

Halo & Ponytail

The Halo Wire sits gravity-based on your head with zero damage. Our professional ponytail extension clips on in under a minute. Both deliver dramatic length and volume with no commitment to your natural hair.

Under 5 min · Zero damage
